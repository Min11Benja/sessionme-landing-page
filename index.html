How is this different from a video conference application?

These tools have their place but they were not designed to let you charge the caller. This is where we think our app can be of use to you. If you are manually charging people before or after for video or audio calls, then now you can have all the great features from those web conference softwares that you already know, with the charging capability handled automatically.

How much does this cost?

We charge a 20% commission per call.

Do I need expensive equipment to update and maintain?

No, thanks to the magic of the internet you can now do this through a normal computer or mobile web browser with a working internet connection, a microphone and a webcam.

Are there any additional hidden fees, or setup costs?

None, we charge a 20% commission per call and that's it! If you are not making money why should we?

How much will I earn per call?

We don't have a maximum fee, but we do have a minimum fee for you to charge, since these calls do cost us money. This means you can earn as much as people are willing to pay you and as little as $1 usd per minute.

When and how do I get paid?

There is a $100 usd minimum amount of money you need to have to be able to ask for a withdrawal. Payments are made on a 8-15 business day after you click on the withdrawal option. These payments can be a direct deposit, check or electronically transferred to a bank account of your choice. We provide you a detailed online daily call count report, so you know exactly how many calls you've received to your account, and we will let you know when and how much money you can withdraw. A custom report is also included with each withdrawal that gives you a complete breakdown of your accounts activity for that period.

How do I keep track of all my calls?

We offer a very intuitive calendar app that lets your clients book calls with you depending on your available time. You can customise this calendar to accept calls on certain days of the week and hours of the day.

What kind of reporting do you offer?

We send you and your caller a receipt via email and you will have access to our professional dashboard with the metrics to help you improve your pay per call service

How will people call me so I can charge them?

You just have to log into our website's application, turn on your microphone or/and webcam, share your unique link to them and accept the call.

Do these calls work internationally?

Right now we only offer these calls in Mexico and the USA but they do work internationally. As long as the other person has a working internet connection and an up to date web browser, microphone and or webcam. These calls should work internationally.

How are taxes handled?

If you are from the US, at the end of the year we will send you a 1099-Misc form so you can report your earnings as ordinary income. If you are outside the US your income will not be reported and no form is needed.

Will there ever be a native IOS or Android app for this?

We would like to but because Apple takes a 30% cut and Google play 15% of revenue, you would end up receiving 50%-65% of what you charged. We think that's way too much, but if people are fine with those fees and keep asking for a native App we will be happy to launch one.

How long does it take to set up my pay per call account?

Just a couple of minutes, we need you to fill out a form that lets us automatically set up your account.

How do I get my pay per call account started?

Just click on the sign up button below and fill out a quick form, we will share with you a unique link you can share in your social media accounts or through any messaging app and you are good to go.
